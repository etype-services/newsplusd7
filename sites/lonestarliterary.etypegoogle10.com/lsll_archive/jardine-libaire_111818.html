ABOUT THE AUTHOR

Jardine Libaire is a graduate of Skidmore College and the University of Michigan MFA program. She is the author of two novels, Here, Kitty, Kitty and White Fur, which was optioned for a television show by Amazon and for which Libaire will be writer on those episodes. She lives in Austin.
